Instructions

Remove the inner bag containing mushroom spawn from the paper packaging.

Cut out instructions on the dotted line. Replace the inner bag into paper packaging.

Cut a roughly 3 inch by 3 inch "X" shape into the plastic spawn bag. This exposes the spawn to air and begins the fruiting process!

Place the kit in a well-lit (but out of direct sunlight!), humid location. Mist three times daily, but try to avoid filling the bag with standing water.

Mushrooms grow best between 68-75Â°F.

Mushrooms are ready to harvest when caps begin to turn upwards (or when you notice that daily growth has slowed rapidly). This process usually takes between 5-12 days.

After harvesting, turn the inner spawn bag around and slice a new opening to begin the process again! Further harvests take longer to begin fruiting, so be patient. Enjoy!

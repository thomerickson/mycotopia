Instructions

1. Remove the inner bag containing mushroom spawn from the paper packaging.

2. Cut out instructions along dotted line. Replace inner bag into packaging.

3. Cut a 3 by 3 inch "X" into exposed inner bag. Be sure to mist the opening ~3 times daily.

Growing Tips

  • Oyster mushrooms grow best in a well-lit, well-ventiliated, and humid environment
  • Avoid placing in direct sunlight
  • For best results, keep between 68-75Â°F

Mushrooms are ready to harvest when caps begin to curl upward (around the time you notice that growth has slowed dramatically, usually between 5-12 days).

After harvesting, turn the inner spawn bag around and cut a new opening to begin growing another batch of mushrooms. Further growth takes a little more time to begin fruiting, so be patient and keep the mushroom spawn moist via misting.

Enjoy!
